ABOUT:

SKI FOODS is an Indian food restaurant chain headquartered in coimbatore that
specializes in
fried chicken. It is the world's second-largest restaurant chain after McDonald's,
with 22,621 locations globally in 150 countries as of December 2019.
The chain is a subsidiary of Yum! Brands, a restaurant company that
also owns the Pizza Hut and Taco Bell chains.
